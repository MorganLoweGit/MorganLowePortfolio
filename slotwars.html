Slot Wars

Summary

  • Brief: Personal project.
  • Timeline: September 2024 - present.
  • "A passion project."

Left click: rotate (hold to pause rotation), scroll wheel: zoom

My role:

During my first semester of my second year at university, I challenged myself to also develop a 3D slot car racing game, using Unreal Engine 5. I took this opportunity to understand spline components and how they can be used in the scene, from the race track, to guardrails, to procedural content generation.

  • Studied spline components, and created a prototype of the track spline actor.
  • Constructing vehicle movement around Unreal Blueprint timelines.
  • Procedurally generating rumble strip/kerbing during corners.
  • Updated a UI system alongside my pipeline.
  • Learnt Unreal Engine's procedural content generation tools to build forests near the racetrack.
  • "AI" opponent actor with a variable difficulty slider.
