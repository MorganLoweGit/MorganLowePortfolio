Glorb Goes Bug Catching

Summary

  • Brief: Global Game Jam 2025 Entry.
  • Timeline: January 2025.
  • "First in-person collaborative game jam."

Left click: rotate (hold to pause rotation), scroll wheel: zoom

My role:

Glorb Goes Bug Catching is a global game jam entry that I developed with 4 team members over the course of two and a half days. I was primarily responsible for level design and the asset creation pipeline.

  • Created 2 3D models, being the bubble gun used by the character and the robot companion.
  • Cleaned and unwrapped said 3D models for texturing.
  • Applied textures to the models in Substance Painter 3D.
  • Managed version control to keep the project organized and up to date.
  • Coordinated with the team to track progress and prioritize tasks.
  • Rigged the robot's wheel tracks for a moving animation.
  • Created the terrain and foliage that the player resides in.
